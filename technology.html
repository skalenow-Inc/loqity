Technology

What is the beacon technology?

The beacons are devices or beacons, that by Bluetooth 4.0 technology, can send messages (push notification) to any smartphone without interruption. Its range is about 50-70 meters away.

The beacons work by proximity. They respond to the proximity between its sensors and smartphones to disseminate information or messages. This technology does not consume battery user as much as other technologies, because it emits its signal to a very low frequency without GPS. It is based on Bluetooth low energy (BLE), also known as Bluetooth Smart.
